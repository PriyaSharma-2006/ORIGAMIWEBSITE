(1) ORIGAMI CAMEL

Fun Facts About Camel
1) Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
2)Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
3)Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.



(2)ORIGAMI PIGEON

Fun Facts About Pigeon
1)Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
2)Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
3)Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.


(3)ORIGAMI ELEPHANT

Fun Facts About Elephant
1)The gestation period of elephants is 22 months. Baby elephants weigh around 100kg on average.
2)The word “elephant” comes from the Greek word “elephas” which means “ivory”.
3)The average lifespan of an elephant is from 50 to 70 years.


(4)ORIGAMI FLYING CICADA

Fun Facts About Flying Cicada
1)The body of a cicada is similar to that of a violin or a guitar.
2)Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.
3)Most have red-orange eyes.
